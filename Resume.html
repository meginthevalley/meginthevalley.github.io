I'm currently focused on job hunting, pursuing relevant certifications, and continuing my volunteer work with NYCWiC (New York Celebration of Women in Computing). As part of this process, I've revamped both my resume and this portfolio to better reflect my evolving interests and experiences. While my primary focus is on opportunities in IT, gaming, and engineering, I've recently expanded my search to include marketing roles—largely inspired by the marketing-related work I've taken on with NYCWiC.

Our 2025 conference was a huge success, and I'm excited to continue contributing as we begin preparing for 2026.
