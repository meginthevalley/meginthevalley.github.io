My name is Megan, I am 24 years old and I have been an avid gamer since I was approximately 4 years old. I may not be the best at video games but I thoroughly enjoy playing them. I like many more games and genres than just the ones listed on this site. Currently, I only usually have the time to play games 2-3 times a week. The reason for this is that I am working part-time and I'm in college. When I do play games you can usually catch me streaming them on twitch. The degree I am working towards is also related to gaming. My major in college is Informatics with a concentration in Game Design and Development. It has always been a dream of mine to work with video games. I'm hoping when I get out of school to make games and voice act in my own video games (being a voice actress is also a dream of mine). Another big thing about me is I'm a huge advocate for mental health. As someone who has struggled with my mental health in the past, it's important to know that there is nothing wrong with struggling with your mental health.
